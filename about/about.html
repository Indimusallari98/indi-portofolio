About me

I am Indi Musallari and I am 21 years old. I was born and raised in the North of Albania in a small town called Shkodra. I have finished an Austrian High School in Shkodra three years ago and I decided to finish my studies in Canada. When I came to Canada I started to study French at UQAM University and I finished my studies at UQAM University last year. Instead of Albanian, that is my mother tongue I can speak English, French and German fluently and I also have knowledge in the Turkish language. Instead of my studies, my passions are fashion design and makeup. My interest in fashion started since I was a little. I always liked to dress up with the latest trends and to combine different designs and colors. Before two years I started to watch makeup tutorials and I opened a personal blog on Instagram to share my interests with my followers. I post pictures on my Instagram every week and I also like to influence my followers with my fashion design and trends.
